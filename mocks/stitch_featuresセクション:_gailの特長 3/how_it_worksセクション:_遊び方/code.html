Turn your inbox into a game

Gail is a new email client that gamifies your inbox. Clear your inbox by defeating waves of unread emails, and level up your productivity.

How to play

Defeat waves of unread emails

Unread emails are represented as waves of enemies. Each wave represents a batch of unread emails.

Attack with replies

Replying to an email is your primary attack. The faster you reply, the more damage you deal.

Use skills with swipes

Swiping emails left or right activates special skills. Each skill has a different effect on the enemy waves.
